Interesting Facts About Camels:

  1. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  2. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  3. There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.

Interesting Facts About Dinosaurs:

  1. Dinosaur fossils have been found on all seven continents.
  2. Modern birds are a kind of dinosaur because they share a common ancestor with non-avian dinosaurs.
  3. In 1842, the English naturalist Sir Richard Owen coined the term Dinosauria, derived from the Greek deinos, meaning “fearfully great,” and sauros, meaning “lizard.”
